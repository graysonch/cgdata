About The Company

CG Data science is a fictitious company created by myself (Christine Grayson) for the optional honors portion of the course "Front-End Web UI Frameworks and Tools: Bootstrap 4" offered by "The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology" through Coursera.

Any resemblance to a real web site it either purely conicidental or because I was encouraged to do an analysis of other similar sites in the design of this project. I did use some words from other web sites just because I wanted the site to look realistic. If this was a real company web site I would not do this.

The course covers a multitude of technologies, including Bootstrap 4, Node.js, Sass/Less, Gulp and Grunt. This prototype web site uses, gulp and Sass.
